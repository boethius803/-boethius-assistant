### Trend Identification

| Term | Meaning | Signal |
|------|---------|--------|
| HH | Higher High | Uptrend confirmed |
| HL | Higher Low | Uptrend intact |
| LH | Lower High | Downtrend confirmed |
| LL | Lower Low | Downtrend intact |

### Structure Shift (BoS)
A shift from bullish‚Üíbearish (or vice) signals potential reversal. The level left behind = **QML (Quasimodo Level)**.

## üî∑ Key Concepts

### Supply & Demand Zones
- **Demand Zone (Green)** = Buying area, institutional accumulation
- **Supply Zone (Red)** = Selling area, institutional distribution
- Wait for price to return to zone + confirmation

### Zone Lifecycle
- **Fresh zone:** untouched, highest probability
- **Tested once:** still valid, moderate
- **Tested twice:** weakened, tread carefully
- **Tested 3x:** likely to break ‚Äî avoid

### Liquidity (LQ)
- Equal highs/lows = liquidity magnets (stop clusters)
- Round numbers = institutional order clusters
- **Liquidity grab = opportunity** (retail stops taken = reversal)

### Pre-Market (30 min)
1. Mark HTF structure (Daily/4H)
2. Identify QMLs, S/D zones
3. Map liquidity pools
4. Set alerts at key levels
5. Define max 3 trade scenarios
