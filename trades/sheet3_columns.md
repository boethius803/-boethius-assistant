# Livermore Columns - Crypto Adaptation

## The 6 Columns

| Column | Livermore | Crypto Adaptation | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| 1 | Secondary Rally | Small bounce in downtrend | <3% move |
| 2 | Natural Rally | Normal up move | 3-6% up |
| **3** | **Upward Trend** | **Confirmed uptrend** | **>6% from low** |
| **4** | **Downward Trend** | **Confirmed downtrend** | **>6% from high** |
| 5 | Natural Reaction | Normal pullback | 3-6% down |
| 6 | Secondary Reaction | Small dip in uptrend | <3% move |

**Rules:**
- Use % moves instead of fixed points
- 6% move shifts between main columns (3↔4)
- 3% move shifts between secondary columns (1↔2, 5↔6)

---

## Recording Rules

### Enter in UPWARD TREND (Column 3) when:
- Price exceeds last Upward Trend level by >3%
- Confirmed by volume >1.5x average

### Shift to NATURAL RALLY (Column 2) when:
- Price drops >3% from Upward Trend high
- Normal pullback, not reversal

### Shift to DOWNWARD TREND (Column 4) when:
- Price drops >6% from high
- OR fails to exceed last Upward Trend by >3%

### Shift to NATURAL REACTION (Column 5) when:
- Price bounces >3% from Downward Trend low
- Normal recovery, not reversal
