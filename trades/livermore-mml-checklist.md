# Livermore-MML Hybrid Trading Checklist

## Pre-Trade: Identify the Trend

| Question | Answer | Action |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Is price ABOVE 4/8 MML line? | → Uptrend: Go LONG | Continue |
| Is price BELOW 4/8 MML line? | → Downtrend: Go SHORT/CASH | Continue |
| Is price in 3/8–5/8 zone? | → No man's land: CASH OUT | STOP |

---

## Step 1: Wait for the Pivotal Point

| Signal | Check |
|--------|-------|
| Price approaching key MML level (0/8, 4/8, 8/8)? | ☐ |
| Breaking out of 5-day range? | ☐ |
| Volume > 1.5x average? | ☐ |

**If ALL checked → Proceed to Step 2**

---

## Step 2: Confirm with Key Price

| Confirmation | Check |
|-------------|-------|
| 60-day range verified from multiple sources? | ☐ |
| Correlated asset confirms (e.g., BTC for alts)? | ☐ |
| Weekly momentum >5% (uptrend) or <-5% (downtrend)? | ☐ |
| Daily green (uptrend) or red (downtrend)? | ☐ |

**If ALL checked → Proceed to Step 3**

---

## Step 3: Enter (Livermore Pyramiding)

| Tranche | Entry Condition | Position Size |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| Initial | At MML level breakout | 10% |
| Add #1 | Price moves +5% in your favor | +10% |
| Add #2 | Price moves +10% in your favor | +10% |
| Runner | Strong continuation | Hold 20% |

**MAX 20% per coin | MAX 5 positions**

---

## Step 4: Exit Strategy (40/40/20)

| Exit | Condition | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| TP1 | First MML level reached | SELL 40% |
| TP2 | Second MML level reached | SELL 40% |
| Runner | Climax / lower high / -5% trail | HOLD/SELL 20% |
| STOP | -5% from entry | EXIT ALL |

---

## Exit Triggers (Livermore Rules)

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Climax (parabolic + volume spike) | EXIT ALL |
| Lower high forms | EXIT ALL |
| Price drops 5% from peak | Trail stop |
| Volume dries up on advance | EXIT |
| Wide-range decline day | EXIT |
| Price crosses below entry MML level | STOP |
